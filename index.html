FAQ

Questions a serious retail buyer may ask.

Do we need a large transformation program to start?

No. The recommended starting point is a focused pilot on one category, region, or business unit with clear baseline KPIs and measurable commercial outcomes.

What data is needed for a pilot?

Typical inputs include sales, price, margin, promotion, inventory, product hierarchy, store/channel, supplier cost, and calendar/event data. The pilot can start with available data and improve progressively.

Is this only a dashboard?

No. The goal is to move beyond reporting into decision recommendations, action tracking, feedback capture, and value measurement.

Can Profinity AI work with existing BI and data platforms?

Yes. The solution can be designed to work with existing cloud, BI, data warehouse, lakehouse, and workflow tools rather than replacing the entire technology landscape.
